Show Picker Club — SMS Messaging

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Show Picker Club is a private, invitation-only TV-show tracker for a small group of friends and family. We use SMS for two things: sending login verification codes, and notifying you when another member recommends or shares a show with you.

How you opt in

SMS is optional. When you join Show Picker Club, you may provide a mobile number to receive login codes and recommendation alerts by text. Members who don't provide a number receive their login code from the group operator directly, so using the service does not require consenting to SMS. The consent language presented at signup reads:

"I agree to receive text messages from Show Picker Club at the number provided, including login verification codes and notifications when another member recommends or shares a show with me. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and Terms."

Consent to receive these messages is not a condition of using Show Picker Club, and there is nothing for sale on the service.

What we send

  • Login codes: a one-time verification code when you sign in.
  • Recommendation alerts: when another member suggests or shares a show with you, a short notice with a link to your list.

Message frequency varies and depends on your own activity and how often others recommend shows to you. Typically a handful of messages per month at most.

Opting out & help

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You'll get one confirmation and then no further messages.
  • Reply START to opt back in.
  • Reply HELP for help, or email patrick@patrickturner.net.

Costs

Show Picker Club does not charge for messages. Message and data rates may apply from your mobile carrier.

Privacy

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing. Phone numbers are used only to operate the app's login and notification features. See the full Privacy Policy.
